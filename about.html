Our History

Tom Johnson started Insure Connect in 1964. It was then named, Hometown Insurance Company.Mr. Tom worked at the head of the helm for more than fifty years, when he turned it over to his sons. The business has grown considerably, but we never forgot our beginning.

Quality Values To Build Upon

Mr. Johnson believed in providing top service and honest answers to customers. This led to the company embracing the values of his upbringing on a farm in Oklahoma. He knew hardworking people needed quality coverage at low prices. This led us to the company we are today.
