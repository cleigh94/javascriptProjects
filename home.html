Grace Saves Us

Grace is unmerited favor and that is what God offers us. You can't earn it. You can't buy it. He gives it freely. We can accept his grace through obedience to His word and through being washed in the blood of Christ, in baptism.

Live Joyfully

Once we accept his grace, are baptized, and washed clean, we can begin to live joyfully with our Lord. He will be there beside you, promising to never leave you nor forsake you.

Promises Kept

What God promises, he keeps. There are no if's or maybe's to it. It is certain and once a person obeys the Gospel call, they can count on salvation. Salvation is promised to all of those who put on our Lord in baptism and live faithfully. Guaranteed!
